# Engineering Doctrine

Immaculate is built to hunt for durable engineering truth, not theater.

## What We Are Optimizing For

- control over intelligence, not mythology about intelligence
- time consistency, replayability, and measurable coordination
- hard system leverage in routing, memory, supervision, and governance
- real capability increases that survive load, failure, and audit

## What Counts As A Breakthrough

- a new capability that changes what the system can actually do
- a reliability boundary crossed that removes a class of failure
- a governance or safety boundary crossed without weakening control
- a benchmark or latency gain that materially changes operating range
- a system truth that was always there but was being missed

## Working Standard

- build boring infrastructure first
- instrument everything that matters
- distrust magic that cannot be replayed
- distrust claims that do not survive benchmarks
- look for hidden bottlenecks in state, timing, routing, and feedback
- when a real leap lands, record it in [[Breakthrough-Log]]

## The Point

Do not aim to imitate the brain literally.

Aim to build a governed, low-latency orchestration substrate that can ingest
real signals, route intelligence under constraint, and evolve without losing
control of state, consent, or causality.
